Indoor Reception - $600/night

The indoor reception hall includes tables, chairs, table clothes, silk flowers, and basic decorations. Additional lights and drapes are available upon request. A small kitchen is included, as well as menu options for catering. All setup can be supervised or changed as needed.

Each reception hall has the same basic package options such as setup. Decorations not provided but requested will be charged at full cost.

Outdoor Reception - $525/night

The outdoor reception hall includes tables, chairs, silk flowers, hanging fairy lights, and basic decorations. Catering and menu options are available. Outdoor table setup cannot be changed, but setup of decorations can be supervised or changed as needed.

Each reception hall has the same basic package options such as setup. Decorations not provided but requested will be charged at full cost.

Package Add-ons

Setup & cleanup - $50

Setup will start two hours before the reception begins. All decorations, tables, chairs, table clothes, and special requests will be set up. After the reception, all items will be cleaned and taken down so that the wedding party and guests can leave at their leisure.

Menu consultation - Free

This is mandatory if the catering service is added to your package. An appetizer, main course, and drinks will be decided at this time. The three basic menus offered are Italian, Rustic, and Caribbean. Additional requests or substitions may be subject to extra cost as evaluated by the consulting agent.

Catering service - $10-$20/person

Catering includes waiters and food preparation. A free menu consultation is mandatory so that appropriate ingredients can be purchased ahead of time. The fee depends on the menu chosen and amount of guests. Every two tables require a waiter.

Fresh flowers - $175

Fresh flowers includes table centerpieces, accents on draperies, and a bridal bouquets. The bouquet can be picked up at any time before or during the reception. A two hours notice of pickup is required. Only one bouquet is created.

Live music - $25/hour

Violin, piano, and cello soloists are available. The musician has a selection of songs that they play as default. Any requests must be made known one week before the reception. If a selection of songs is desired, these must be mailed to the musician two weeks before the reception.
