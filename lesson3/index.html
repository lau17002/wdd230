Site Purpose

Business professionals and thought leaders who are currently engaged in business or would like to conduct business in the Rexburg area are invited to team up with us. We are creating an environment in which a business can prosper by providing planning, inspiration, and guidance. Futhering the progess and prosperity of small businesses is our main purpose. We do this by listing all of the businesses within Rexburg and by providing a calendar of events. We will also put together events for opputrunities to donate to charity or to do service for the community.
