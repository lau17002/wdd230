Contrast

Apple

apple.com

Contrast is the difference between the colors of a website while enhancing user readability. Apple uses a white background to make the images of the phones pop. It also uses blank font with a white background and a white font with a black background.

Repetition

Crumbl

crumblcookies.com

Repetition refers to using the same structure, font, and colors repeatedly to make the page feel cohesive. Crumbl maintains a black and white theme throughout their main page. To add color, they display their cookies all in the same angle and display the same type of information for each cookie.

Rule of Thirds

LinkedIn

linkedin.com

The Rule of Thirds is to simply apply a grid style layout to the page or picture. LinkedIn provides this layout by having three boxes lined up vertically. When the page is expanded, you will see that the image lines up with these boxes and creates a grid between the content and the image of the page.
